A chill drinking game for whoever's still answering the group chat.

TAP TO BEGIN
Pass the phone clockwise. Whoever taps the deck reads the card aloud — and it's about them. Skip a card, take a sip.
House rules
  • Skip a card, 1 sip.
  • Refuse a dare, 2 sips.
  • Caught lying on a question, 3 sips.
  • Drink water between rounds.
  • No one drives. Look out for each other.
How to play

Three rules,
one circle.

01
Sit in a circle. Pass the phone clockwise after each card. The person holding the phone is the one in the spotlight.
02
Tap the deck. A question or a dare appears. Answer honestly, do the dare, or sip to skip — your call.
03
Pass it on. Reset the room with a sip of water every five rounds. The night is long.
